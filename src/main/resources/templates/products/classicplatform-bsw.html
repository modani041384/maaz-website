Basic Software (BSW)

MaaZ Basic Software provides a full AUTOSAR solution, including AUTOSAR R4-compliant basic software, taking security to the highest level with safety-critical ECUs.


While adapting AUTOSAR, to maximize productivity and reusability, as well as to meet up with the demands of automobile manufacturers, the customers continuously build layers on the premise of software generalization. Nevertheless, these projects will require huge and expert technical resources.

With our basic software solution for Classic Platform, MaaZ transforms how you adapt AUTOSAR. MaaZ divides the roles of the application layer and platform layer, utilizing legacy assets along with AUTOSAR modules.

We also conduct a computer-based risk assessment by Fit & Gap, architecture design, configuration, and skill transfer with the consideration of the derivative development or lateral deployment time, which enhances the performance of the AUTOSAR transformation.
